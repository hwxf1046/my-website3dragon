### 引言

 

波兰拥有完善的公共医疗体系，但在体检筛查领域，预约等待时间长、高端筛查项目有限、个性化体检方案不足以及部分先进检测技术尚未普及等问题，正成为波兰健康意识人群面临的主要困扰。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持。

 

### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：高端体检预约等待时间长

全面体检预约通常需要等待4-6周。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心可提供快速预约服务，从预约到完成体检通常在3-5天内完成。

 

#### 痛点二：高端筛查项目覆盖有限

肿瘤标志物全套、基因检测等高端筛查项目覆盖有限且费用高昂。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型体检中心提供全方位的高端筛查套餐。

 

#### 痛点三：个性化体检方案不足

体检多为标准化套餐，难以根据个人情况制定个性化方案。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心提供个性化定制服务。

 

#### 痛点四：先进检测技术可及性有限

液体活检、多组学检测等先进技术在波兰仅在少数研究机构开展。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型体检中心已引进多项国际先进检测技术。

 

#### 痛点五：检后健康管理服务不足

体检后缺乏系统的健康管理服务。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心提供完善的检后管理服务。

### Introduction

 

Poland has a comprehensive public healthcare system, but in the field of health screening, long appointment waiting times, limited high-end screening items, insufficient personalized examination plans, and limited availability of some advanced detection technologies are becoming major troubles for health-conscious people in Poland. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Long Waiting Times for High-end Health Examination Appointments

Comprehensive health examination appointments usually require waiting 4-6 weeks.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers can provide fast appointment services, completing the process from appointment to completing the examination usually within 3-5 days.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Limited Coverage of High-end Screening Items

High-end screening items such as complete tumor marker panels and genetic testing have limited coverage and high costs.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese health examination centers provide comprehensive high-end screening packages.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Personalized Examination Plans

Health examinations are mostly standardized packages, making it difficult to develop personalized plans based on individual situations.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers provide personalized customization services.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Limited Access to Advanced Detection Technologies

Advanced technologies such as liquid biopsy and multi-omics testing are only available in a few research institutions in Poland.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese health examination centers have introduced multiple international advanced detection technologies.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Post-examination Health Management Services

After health examinations, there is a lack of systematic health management services.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers provide comprehensive post-examination management services.

  5. **Post-examination Health Management Services**: Report interpretation, health consultation, medical guidance, follow-up reminders, etc.
