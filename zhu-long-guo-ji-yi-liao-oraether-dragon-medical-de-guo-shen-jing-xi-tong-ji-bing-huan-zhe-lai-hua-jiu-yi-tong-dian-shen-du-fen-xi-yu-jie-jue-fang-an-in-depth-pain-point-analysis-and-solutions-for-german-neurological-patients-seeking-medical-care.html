### 德国神经系统疾病患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：神经专科医生严重短缺

 

根据德国神经病学学会数据，德国每10万人口仅有约8名神经科医生，远低于实际需求。患者从初诊到获得专科医生面诊平均需要等待8-12周，对于帕金森病、阿尔茨海默病等进展性疾病，这种延误可能导致不可逆的病情恶化。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型三甲医院神经内科医生资源相对充足，可快速安排专家面诊。烛龙医疗与北京天坛医院、上海华山医院等国内顶级神经专科医院合作，确保患者在1周内获得权威专家诊疗。

 

#### 痛点二：创新药物和疗法审批滞后

 

欧盟药品审批流程复杂漫长，许多在美国或亚洲已上市的神经疾病新药在德国需要额外2-3年才能获批。对于罕见神经系统疾病患者，这意味着错过最佳治疗时机。

 

**解决方案：** 中国近年来加快创新药审批速度，多家医院参与国际多中心临床试验，患者可同步获得全球最新治疗方案。烛龙医疗可协助评估患者是否符合临床试验入组条件，加速新药可及性。

 

#### 痛点三：康复治疗资源不足

 

德国神经康复体系虽完善，但优质康复机构数量有限，等待入住时间长达数月。对于脑卒中、脊髓损伤等需要早期密集康复的患者，等待意味着功能恢复机会的丧失。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级神经专科医院配备现代化康复中心，可提供早期、密集、系统的康复治疗。烛龙医疗可协助安排患者入院后立即开始康复训练，最大化功能恢复潜力。

 

#### 痛点四：复杂手术费用高昂

 

德国私立医院的神经外科手术费用极高，一次脑肿瘤切除手术费用可达5-8万欧元，功能性神经外科手术后费用更高。即使有保险覆盖，自付部分仍给家庭带来沉重负担。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶尖神经外科中心的手术费用仅为德国的1/3至1/2，且手术质量和安全性达到国际先进水平。烛龙医疗帮助患者在保证治疗效果的同时大幅降低经济压力。

 

#### 痛点五：多学科协作诊疗困难

 

神经系统疾病往往需要神经内科、神经外科、康复科、心理科等多学科协作。德国医疗体系分工明确但协调困难，患者需要在不同科室间奔波，影响诊疗效率。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型神经专科医院实行MDT多学科会诊制度，相关科室专家共同为患者制定综合治疗方案。烛龙医疗协助安排MDT会诊，确保患者获得最优化的诊疗策略。

### Introduction

### Core Pain Point Analysis for German Neurological Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Severe Shortage of Neurology Specialists

 

According to the German Neurological Society, Germany has only about 8 neurologists per 100,000 population, far below actual demand. Patients need to wait an average of 8-12 weeks from initial consultation to seeing a specialist. For progressive diseases like Parkinson's disease and Alzheimer's disease, this delay may lead to irreversible disease deterioration.

 

**Solution:** Neurologist resources in large Chinese tertiary hospitals are relatively sufficient, and specialist consultations can be arranged quickly. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top neurological specialty hospitals such as Beijing Tiantan Hospital and Shanghai Huashan Hospital to ensure patients receive authoritative expert diagnosis and treatment within 1 week.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Lag in Approval of Innovative Drugs and Therapies

 

The EU drug approval process is complex and lengthy. Many new neurological drugs already marketed in the US or Asia require an additional 2-3 years to be approved in Germany. For patients with rare neurological diseases, this means missing the optimal treatment timing.

 

**Solution:** China has accelerated innovative drug approval in recent years, with multiple hospitals participating in international multi-center clinical trials, allowing patients to access the latest global treatment plans simultaneously. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in evaluating whether patients meet clinical trial enrollment criteria, accelerating access to new drugs.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Rehabilitation Resources

 

Although Germany's neurological rehabilitation system is comprehensive, the number of high-quality rehabilitation institutions is limited, with waiting times for admission reaching several months. For patients requiring early intensive rehabilitation such as stroke and spinal cord injury, waiting means loss of functional recovery opportunities.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese neurological specialty hospitals are equipped with modern rehabilitation centers that can provide early, intensive, and systematic rehabilitation treatment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in arranging patients to start rehabilitation training immediately after admission, maximizing functional recovery potential.

 

#### Pain Point 4: High Costs of Complex Surgeries

 

Neurosurgical procedure costs in German private hospitals are extremely high. A brain tumor resection surgery can cost €50,000-80,000, and functional neurosurgery costs are even higher. Even with insurance coverage, the out-of-pocket portion still brings heavy burden to families.

 

**Solution:** Surgical costs at top Chinese neurosurgical centers are only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Germany, while surgical quality and safety have reached international advanced levels. Aether Dragon Medical helps patients significantly reduce financial pressure while ensuring treatment effectiveness.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Difficulty in Multidisciplinary Collaborative Diagnosis and Treatment

 

Neurological diseases often require multidisciplinary collaboration among neurology, neurosurgery, rehabilitation, and psychology departments. The German healthcare system has clear division but difficult coordination, requiring patients to rush between different departments, affecting diagnostic and treatment efficiency.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese neurological specialty hospitals implement MDT (Multidisciplinary Team) consultation systems, where experts from relevant departments jointly develop comprehensive treatment plans for patients. Aether Dragon Medical assists in arranging MDT consultations to ensure patients receive optimal diagnostic and treatment strategies.
