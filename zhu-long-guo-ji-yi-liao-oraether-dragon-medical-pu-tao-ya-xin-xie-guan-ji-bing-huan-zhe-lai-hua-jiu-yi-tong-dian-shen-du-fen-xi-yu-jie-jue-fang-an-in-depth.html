### 引言

 

葡萄牙拥有完善的公共医疗体系，但在心血管疾病治疗领域，等待时间长、先进介入治疗可及性有限、心脏康复资源不足以及部分复杂心脏手术经验有限等问题，正成为葡萄牙心血管患者面临的主要困扰。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持，我们不诊断、不治疗，只做一件事：做你在中国的"医疗拐杖+翻译管家+全程向导"。

 

### 葡萄牙心血管患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：公立医疗等待时间过长

 

葡萄牙国家医疗服务体系（SNS）下，心血管专科门诊和手术等待时间普遍较长。根据葡萄牙卫生部数据，冠脉搭桥手术平均等待时间为4-6个月，心脏瓣膜手术等待时间甚至更长。对于病情危急的患者，这种延误可能是致命的。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级心血管中心可提供快速就诊通道，从初诊到手术通常在2周内完成。烛龙医疗与北京阜外医院、上海中山医院等国内顶尖心血管专科医院合作，确保患者及时获得治疗。

 

#### 痛点二：先进介入治疗技术普及度不高

 

葡萄牙部分先进心血管介入治疗技术（如TAVR、MitraClip等）仅在少数中心开展，且准入标准严格，许多患者无法及时获得这些微创治疗方案。

 

**解决方案：** 中国在心血管介入治疗领域发展迅速，多家医院年手术量位居世界前列，医生经验丰富。烛龙医疗可帮助患者对接开展各类先进介入治疗的医院。

 

#### 痛点三：心脏康复服务覆盖有限

 

葡萄牙心脏康复项目覆盖有限，许多患者出院后缺乏系统的康复指导和随访管理，影响长期预后和生活质量。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型心血管中心提供完善的术后康复体系，包括运动康复、营养指导、心理支持等多维度服务。烛龙医疗可协助安排患者进行系统化康复治疗。

 

#### 痛点四：复杂心脏手术经验不足

 

对于复杂先天性心脏病矫正、再次心脏手术等高难度手术，葡萄牙能够开展的医院有限，医生经验相对不足。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级心血管中心年手术量巨大，医生积累了丰富的复杂病例处理经验。烛龙医疗可协助患者对接擅长复杂心脏手术的专家团队。

 

#### 痛点五：私立医疗费用高昂

 

选择私立医院可缩短等待时间，但葡萄牙私立心血管治疗费用极高，一次心脏手术费用可达2-4万欧元，普通家庭难以承担。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶尖医院的心血管手术费用仅为葡萄牙的1/3至1/2，且医疗质量达到国际先进水平。烛龙医疗帮助患者在保证治疗效果的同时大幅降低经济负担。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Portuguese Cardiovascular Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times in Public Healthcare

 

Under Portugal's National Health Service (SNS), waiting times for cardiovascular specialist outpatient visits and surgeries are generally long. According to the Portuguese Ministry of Health, the average waiting time for coronary artery bypass graft surgery is 4-6 months, and heart valve surgery waiting time is even longer. For patients with critical conditions, this delay can be fatal.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese cardiovascular centers can provide fast-track medical services, completing the process from initial consultation to surgery usually within 2 weeks. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top cardiovascular specialty hospitals such as Beijing Fuwai Hospital and Shanghai Zhongshan Hospital to ensure patients receive timely treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Low Popularity of Advanced Interventional Treatment Technologies

 

Some advanced cardiovascular interventional treatment technologies in Portugal (such as TAVR, MitraClip, etc.) are only available in a few centers with strict access criteria, preventing many patients from obtaining these minimally invasive treatment plans promptly.

 

**Solution:** China has developed rapidly in the field of cardiovascular interventional therapy, with multiple hospitals ranking among the top worldwide in annual surgical volume and doctors having rich experience. Aether Dragon Medical can help patients connect with hospitals conducting various advanced interventional treatments.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Coverage of Cardiac Rehabilitation Services

 

Cardiac rehabilitation program coverage in Portugal is limited, and many patients lack systematic rehabilitation guidance and follow-up management after discharge, affecting long-term prognosis and quality of life.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese cardiovascular centers provide comprehensive postoperative rehabilitation systems, including multi-dimensional services such as exercise rehabilitation, nutritional guidance, and psychological support. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in arranging systematic rehabilitation treatment for patients.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Insufficient Experience in Complex Cardiac Surgeries

 

For high-difficulty surgeries such as complex congenital heart disease correction and repeat cardiac surgeries, the number of hospitals capable of performing these procedures in Portugal is limited, and doctor experience is relatively insufficient.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese cardiovascular centers have huge annual surgical volumes, and doctors have accumulated rich experience in handling complex cases. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with expert teams specializing in complex cardiac surgeries.

 

#### Pain Point 5: High Private Medical Costs

 

Choosing private hospitals can shorten waiting times, but private cardiovascular treatment costs in Portugal are extremely high. A cardiac surgery can cost €20,000-40,000, making it unaffordable for ordinary families.

 

**Solution:** Cardiovascular surgery costs at top Chinese hospitals are only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Portugal, while medical quality has reached international advanced levels. Aether Dragon Medical helps patients significantly reduce financial burden while ensuring treatment effectiveness.
