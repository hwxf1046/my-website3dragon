### 芬兰中医治疗患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：合格中医师极度短缺

 

芬兰注册中医师数量极少，全国仅有数十名持证中医师，且主要集中在赫尔辛基等大城市。农村和偏远地区居民几乎无法获得正规中医服务，需要长途跋涉至大城市就诊。

 

**解决方案：** 中国是中医的发源地，拥有最权威的中医师资源和最完善的中医诊疗体系。烛龙医疗与北京中医药大学东直门医院、上海中医药大学附属龙华医院等国内顶尖中医院合作，确保患者获得正宗中医诊疗。

 

#### 痛点二：中药获取困难且质量难保证

 

芬兰对中药进口有严格限制，许多常用中药材无法合法进口。即使能获得，药材质量也难以保证，影响治疗效果。中成药在芬兰基本无法获得。

 

**解决方案：** 在中国就诊可直接使用优质中药材和中成药，药材来源可靠、炮制规范。烛龙医疗可协助患者根据医嘱购买正规渠道的中药，并可协助办理合法携带出境手续。

 

#### 痛点三：中医诊疗体系不完整

 

芬兰的中医服务多为零散的针灸或按摩服务，缺乏完整的中医辨证论治体系。患者难以获得系统的中医诊断和综合治疗方案。

 

**解决方案：** 中国中医院提供完整的中医诊疗服务，包括望闻问切四诊合参、辨证论治、中药汤剂、针灸推拿等综合治疗。烛龙医疗协助患者获得系统性的中医诊疗。

 

#### 痛点四：等待时间长

 

即使在大城市，芬兰知名中医师的预约也需要等待数周至数月。对于希望尽快缓解症状的患者，这种等待是巨大的困扰。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型中医院可快速安排就诊，从初诊到开始治疗通常在1周内完成。烛龙医疗与多家知名中医院合作，确保患者及时获得治疗。

 

#### 痛点五：语言与文化障碍

 

芬兰本地中医师多为外籍，中文水平有限，难以准确理解中医经典理论和术语。患者也难以获得纯正的中医文化体验。

 

**解决方案：** 在中国就诊可直接与母语为中文的中医师沟通，准确传达病情。烛龙医疗提供专业医学翻译服务，帮助芬兰患者更好地理解中医理论和治疗方案。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Finnish TCM Treatment Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Extreme Shortage of Qualified TCM Practitioners

 

The number of registered TCM practitioners in Finland is extremely small, with only dozens of licensed TCM practitioners nationwide, mainly concentrated in big cities like Helsinki. Residents in rural and remote areas can hardly obtain formal TCM services and need to travel long distances to big cities for medical visits.

 

**Solution:** China is the birthplace of TCM, possessing the most authoritative TCM practitioner resources and the most complete TCM diagnosis and treatment system. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top TCM hospitals such as Beijing University of Chinese Medicine Dongzhimen Hospital and Shanghai University of Traditional Chinese Medicine Affiliated Longhua Hospital to ensure patients receive authentic TCM diagnosis and treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Difficulty in Obtaining Chinese Herbal Medicines with Uncertain Quality

 

Finland has strict restrictions on Chinese herbal medicine imports, and many commonly used Chinese herbal materials cannot be legally imported. Even if obtained, herb quality is difficult to guarantee, affecting treatment outcomes. Chinese patent medicines are basically unavailable in Finland.

 

**Solution:** Receiving treatment in China allows direct use of high-quality Chinese herbal materials and patent medicines, with reliable sources and standardized processing. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in purchasing Chinese medicines from formal channels according to medical advice and assist in handling legal procedures for taking them out of the country.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Incomplete TCM Diagnosis and Treatment System

 

TCM services in Finland are mostly scattered acupuncture or massage services, lacking a complete TCM syndrome differentiation and treatment system. Patients find it difficult to obtain systematic TCM diagnosis and comprehensive treatment plans.

 

**Solution:** Chinese TCM hospitals provide complete TCM diagnosis and treatment services, including the four diagnostic methods (inspection, listening/smelling, inquiry, and pulse-taking), syndrome differentiation and treatment, Chinese herbal decoctions, acupuncture, tuina, and other comprehensive treatments. Aether Dragon Medical assists patients in obtaining systematic TCM diagnosis and treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Long Waiting Times

 

Even in big cities, appointments with well-known Finnish TCM practitioners require waiting several weeks to months. For patients hoping to relieve symptoms as soon as possible, this waiting is a tremendous trouble.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese TCM hospitals can quickly arrange medical visits, completing the process from initial consultation to starting treatment usually within 1 week. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with multiple well-known TCM hospitals to ensure patients receive timely treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Language and Cultural Barriers

 

Most local Finnish TCM practitioners are foreigners with limited Chinese proficiency, making it difficult to accurately understand TCM classical theories and terminology. Patients also find it difficult to obtain an authentic TCM cultural experience.

 

**Solution:** Receiving treatment in China allows direct communication with Chinese-speaking TCM practitioners, accurately conveying medical conditions. Aether Dragon Medical provides professional medical translation services to help Finnish patients better understand TCM theories and treatment plans.
