### 引言

 

奥地利拥有高质量的医疗服务体系，但在罕见病诊疗领域，专科医生短缺、诊断周期长、治疗药物可及性有限以及部分罕见病缺乏有效治疗方案等问题，正成为奥地利罕见病患者面临的主要挑战。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持，我们不诊断、不治疗，只做一件事：做你在中国的"医疗拐杖+翻译管家+全程向导"。

 

### 奥地利罕见病诊疗患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：罕见病诊断周期过长

 

奥地利罕见病诊断平均需要2-5年，患者往往需要辗转多个科室和医院才能获得确诊。漫长的诊断过程不仅延误治疗时机，还给患者和家庭带来巨大的心理和经济负担。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院设有罕见病多学科诊疗中心，配备先进的基因检测和代谢组学检测设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，将诊断时间缩短至数周。

 

#### 痛点二：专科医生资源极度稀缺

 

奥地利罕见病专科医生数量有限，且主要集中在维也纳等大城市。农村和偏远地区患者难以获得专业诊疗服务，需要长途跋涉至大城市就诊。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型罕见病诊疗中心汇聚了国内最顶尖的罕见病专家，涵盖遗传代谢病、神经肌肉病、罕见肿瘤等多个亚专科。烛龙医疗可协助患者对接权威专家团队。

 

#### 痛点三：治疗药物可及性有限

 

许多罕见病特效药在奥地利尚未获批上市，或虽已获批但价格极其昂贵，患者难以获得。部分药物需要从国外特殊进口，手续复杂且等待时间长。

 

**解决方案：** 中国近年来加快罕见病药物审批速度，多家医院参与国际多中心临床试验，患者可同步获得全球最新治疗方案。烛龙医疗可协助评估患者是否符合临床试验入组条件。

 

#### 痛点四：多学科协作诊疗困难

 

罕见病往往涉及多个系统，需要多学科协作诊疗。奥地利医疗体系分工明确但协调困难，患者需要在不同科室间奔波，影响诊疗效率。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型罕见病中心实行MDT多学科会诊制度，相关科室专家共同为患者制定综合治疗方案。烛龙医疗协助安排MDT会诊，确保患者获得最优化的诊疗策略。

 

#### 痛点五：长期随访管理不足

 

罕见病需要长期随访和管理，但奥地利部分地区缺乏完善的罕见病随访体系，患者出院后难以获得持续的医疗支持和指导。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级罕见病中心提供长期随访管理服务，包括定期复查提醒、用药指导、并发症预防等。烛龙医疗可协助建立患者与国内医生的长期联系。

### Introduction

 

Austria has a high-quality healthcare service system, but in the field of rare disease diagnosis and treatment, shortage of specialist doctors, long diagnostic cycles, limited access to treatment drugs, and lack of effective treatment plans for some rare diseases are becoming major challenges for Austrian rare disease patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China. We do not diagnose or treat; we do one thing only: be your "medical crutch + translation butler + full-process guide" in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Austrian Rare Disease Diagnosis and Treatment Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessively Long Rare Disease Diagnostic Cycles

 

Rare disease diagnosis in Austria takes an average of 2-5 years. Patients often need to visit multiple departments and hospitals before obtaining a confirmed diagnosis. The lengthy diagnostic process not only delays treatment timing but also brings tremendous psychological and financial burden to patients and families.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have established rare disease multidisciplinary diagnosis and treatment centers, equipped with advanced genetic testing and metabolomics detection equipment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations, shortening diagnostic time to several weeks.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Extremely Scarce Specialist Doctor Resources

 

The number of rare disease specialist doctors in Austria is limited and mainly concentrated in big cities like Vienna. Patients in rural and remote areas find it difficult to obtain professional diagnostic and treatment services and need to travel long distances to big cities for medical visits.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese rare disease diagnosis and treatment centers gather the top rare disease experts in China, covering multiple subspecialties such as genetic metabolic diseases, neuromuscular diseases, and rare tumors. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with authoritative expert teams.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Access to Treatment Drugs

 

Many rare disease-specific drugs have not yet been approved for marketing in Austria, or although approved, prices are extremely expensive, making them inaccessible to patients. Some drugs need to be specially imported from abroad, with complex procedures and long waiting times.

 

**Solution:** China has accelerated rare disease drug approval in recent years, with multiple hospitals participating in international multi-center clinical trials, allowing patients to access the latest global treatment plans simultaneously. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in evaluating whether patients meet clinical trial enrollment criteria.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Multidisciplinary Collaborative Diagnosis and Treatment

 

Rare diseases often involve multiple systems and require multidisciplinary collaborative diagnosis and treatment. The Austrian healthcare system has clear division but difficult coordination, requiring patients to rush between different departments, affecting diagnostic and treatment efficiency.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese rare disease centers implement MDT (Multidisciplinary Team) consultation systems, where experts from relevant departments jointly develop comprehensive treatment plans for patients. Aether Dragon Medical assists in arranging MDT consultations to ensure patients receive optimal diagnostic and treatment strategies.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Long-term Follow-up Management

 

Rare diseases require long-term follow-up and management, but some areas in Austria lack complete rare disease follow-up systems, making it difficult for patients to obtain continuous medical support and guidance after discharge.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese rare disease centers provide long-term follow-up management services, including regular follow-up reminders, medication guidance, complication prevention, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in establishing long-term connections between patients and domestic doctors.

#### 2. Green Channel for Fast Diagnosis

#### 3. Professional Rare Disease Medical Translation Team

#### 4. Clinical Trial Enrollment Assistance

 

Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in evaluating whether they meet rare disease clinical trial enrollment criteria and assist in completing the application process, giving patients opportunities to access the latest treatment plans.

 

#### 5. Transparent and Reasonable Costs
