Pain Point Analysis | 痛点分析

International Coordination and Post-Operative Management for Organ Transplantation

器官移植的跨国协调与术后管理

CHALLENGE: Patients needing organ transplantation face complex issues including donor matching, cross-border legal compliance, and post-operative anti-rejection therapy, unaware of Chinas organ allocation system and transplant center capabilities.

International patients face language barriers, unfamiliarity with Chinese medical systems, difficulty verifying credentials, concerns about quality standards, logistical complexities, cultural differences, anxiety about treatment far from home, cost uncertainty, and lack of post-treatment follow-up support.

挑战：需要器官移植的患者面临供体匹配、跨境法律合规、术后抗排斥治疗等复杂问题，不了解中国器官分配系统和移植中心的技术水平。

国际患者面临语言障碍、不熟悉中国医疗体系、难以验证资质、担心质量标准、后勤复杂性、文化差异、远离家乡治疗的焦虑、费用不确定性和缺乏治疗后随访支持。
