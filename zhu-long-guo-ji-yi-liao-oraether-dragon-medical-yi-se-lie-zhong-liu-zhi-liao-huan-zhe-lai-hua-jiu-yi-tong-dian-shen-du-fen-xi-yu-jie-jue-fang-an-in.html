### 引言

 

以色列拥有世界领先的医疗技术，尤其在肿瘤治疗领域。然而，高昂的治疗费用、新药可及性有限、等待时间长以及部分罕见肿瘤诊疗资源不足等问题，正成为以色列肿瘤患者面临的主要困扰。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持，我们不诊断、不治疗，只做一件事：做你在中国的"医疗拐杖+翻译管家+全程向导"。

 

### 以色列肿瘤患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：靶向治疗和免疫治疗费用极高

 

以色列虽有多款靶向药和免疫药物上市，但费用极其昂贵。一次PD-1抑制剂治疗费用可达数千美元，长期治疗给家庭带来沉重经济负担。部分新药尚未纳入医保，患者需完全自费。

 

**解决方案：** 中国已引进多款国际主流靶向药和免疫药物，且价格仅为以色列的1/3至1/2。多家医院参与国际多中心临床试验，患者有机会免费获得最新药物治疗。烛龙医疗可协助评估患者是否符合临床试验入组条件。

 

#### 痛点二：质子重离子治疗可及性有限

 

以色列质子治疗中心数量有限，预约等待时间长，且费用高昂。对于需要精准放疗的患者，这种限制可能影响治疗效果。

 

**解决方案：** 中国已建成多家质子重离子治疗中心，设备先进、经验丰富。烛龙医疗可协助患者对接相关中心，获得精准的放射治疗方案。

 

#### 痛点三：罕见肿瘤诊疗经验不足

 

对于某些罕见肿瘤类型，以色列能够确诊和治疗的中心有限，医生经验相对不足，患者往往需要出国寻求第二诊疗意见。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型肿瘤中心年接诊量大，医生积累了丰富的罕见肿瘤诊疗经验。烛龙医疗可协助患者对接权威专家团队，获得更可靠的诊疗方案。

 

#### 痛点四：中西医结合治疗资源缺乏

 

以色列以西医治疗为主，缺乏中医药辅助治疗资源。许多患者希望采用中西医结合方式减轻放化疗副作用，但难以获得正规中医服务。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级肿瘤医院提供中西医结合治疗服务，可在西医治疗基础上配合中医药调理，减轻副作用、提高生活质量。烛龙医疗协助患者获得综合治疗方案。

 

#### 痛点五：术后康复和姑息治疗不足

 

以色列肿瘤术后康复和姑息治疗资源有限，患者出院后缺乏系统的康复指导和疼痛管理，影响生活质量和预后。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型肿瘤中心提供完善的术后康复和姑息治疗服务，包括疼痛管理、营养支持、心理疏导等。烛龙医疗可协助患者获得全面的 supportive care。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Israeli Cancer Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Extremely High Costs of Targeted and Immunotherapy

 

Although Israel has multiple targeted drugs and immunotherapies available, costs are extremely expensive. A single PD-1 inhibitor treatment can cost thousands of US dollars, and long-term treatment brings heavy financial burden to families. Some new drugs are not yet included in health insurance, requiring patients to pay entirely out-of-pocket.

 

**Solution:** China has introduced multiple international mainstream targeted drugs and immunotherapies, with prices only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Israel. Multiple hospitals participate in international multi-center clinical trials, giving patients opportunities to receive the latest drug treatments for free. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in evaluating whether patients meet clinical trial enrollment criteria.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Limited Access to Proton and Heavy Ion Therapy

 

Israel has limited proton therapy centers, long appointment waiting times, and high costs. For patients requiring precise radiotherapy, this limitation may affect treatment outcomes.

 

**Solution:** China has built multiple proton and heavy ion therapy centers with advanced equipment and rich experience. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with relevant centers to obtain precise radiation treatment plans.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Experience in Rare Tumor Diagnosis and Treatment

 

For certain rare tumor types, the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in Israel is limited, and doctor experience is relatively insufficient. Patients often need to go abroad to seek second opinions.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese cancer centers have high annual patient volumes, and doctors have accumulated rich experience in diagnosing and treating rare tumors. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with authoritative expert teams to obtain more reliable diagnostic and treatment plans.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Lack of Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Resources

 

Israel mainly focuses on Western medicine treatment and lacks traditional Chinese medicine adjunctive therapy resources. Many patients hope to adopt integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine approaches to reduce chemotherapy and radiotherapy side effects but find it difficult to obtain formal TCM services.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese cancer hospitals provide integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine treatment services, combining TCM conditioning with Western medicine treatment to reduce side effects and improve quality of life. Aether Dragon Medical assists patients in obtaining comprehensive treatment plans.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Postoperative Rehabilitation and Palliative Care

 

Israel has limited resources for cancer postoperative rehabilitation and palliative care. Patients lack systematic rehabilitation guidance and pain management after discharge, affecting quality of life and prognosis.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese cancer centers provide comprehensive postoperative rehabilitation and palliative care services, including pain management, nutritional support, psychological counseling, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in obtaining comprehensive supportive care.
