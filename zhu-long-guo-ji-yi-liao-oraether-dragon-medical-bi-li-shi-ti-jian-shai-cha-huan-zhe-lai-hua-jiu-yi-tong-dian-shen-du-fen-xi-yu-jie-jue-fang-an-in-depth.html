### 比利时体检筛查患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：高端体检预约等待时间长

 

比利时公共医疗体系下，全面体检预约通常需要等待4-8周，部分热门医疗机构等待时间更长。对于希望及时了解健康状况的人群，这种延误可能错过早期发现疾病的最佳时机。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心可提供快速预约服务，从预约到完成体检通常在3-5天内完成。烛龙医疗与爱康国宾、美年大健康、北京协和医院体检中心等国内顶尖体检机构合作，确保客户及时获得全面体检。

 

#### 痛点二：高端筛查项目覆盖有限

 

比利时常规体检项目较为基础，对于肿瘤标志物全套、基因检测、心脑血管深度评估等高端筛查项目覆盖有限，且费用高昂。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型体检中心提供全方位的高端筛查套餐，包括肿瘤早筛、基因检测、心脑血管风险评估、免疫功能评估等。烛龙医疗可协助客户选择适合的体检套餐，获得更全面的健康评估。

 

#### 痛点三：个性化体检方案不足

 

比利时体检多为标准化套餐，难以根据个人年龄、性别、家族史、生活习惯等因素制定个性化体检方案，导致部分重要检查项目遗漏或过度检查。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心提供个性化定制服务，根据客户具体情况量身定制体检方案。烛龙医疗协助客户与体检专家沟通，制定最适合的体检计划。

 

#### 痛点四：先进检测技术可及性有限

 

部分先进检测技术（如液体活检、多组学检测、AI影像辅助诊断等）在比利时仅在少数研究机构开展，普通民众难以获得这些前沿检测服务。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型体检中心已引进多项国际先进检测技术，包括液体活检癌症早筛、多组学健康评估、AI医学影像分析等。烛龙医疗可帮助客户获得这些前沿检测服务。

 

#### 痛点五：检后健康管理服务不足

 

比利时体检后缺乏系统的健康管理服务，体检报告解读简单，缺乏后续的健康干预指导和跟踪随访，体检价值未能充分发挥。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级体检中心提供完善的检后管理服务，包括专业报告解读、健康风险评估、个性化健康干预方案、定期随访等。烛龙医疗可协助客户建立长期健康管理档案。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Belgian Health Screening Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Long Waiting Times for High-end Health Examination Appointments

 

Under Belgium's public healthcare system, comprehensive health examination appointments usually require waiting 4-8 weeks, with even longer waiting times at some popular medical institutions. For people hoping to understand their health status promptly, this delay may miss the optimal timing for early disease detection.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers can provide fast appointment services, completing the process from appointment to completing the examination usually within 3-5 days. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top health examination institutions such as iKang Guobin, Meinian Onehealth, Peking Union Medical College Hospital Health Examination Center to ensure clients receive timely comprehensive examinations.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Limited Coverage of High-end Screening Items

 

Routine health examination items in Belgium are relatively basic, with limited coverage of high-end screening items such as complete tumor marker panels, genetic testing, and in-depth cardiovascular and cerebrovascular assessments, and costs are high.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese health examination centers provide comprehensive high-end screening packages, including early cancer screening, genetic testing, cardiovascular and cerebrovascular risk assessment, immune function assessment, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist clients in selecting suitable examination packages to obtain more comprehensive health assessments.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Personalized Examination Plans

 

Health examinations in Belgium are mostly standardized packages, making it difficult to develop personalized examination plans based on individual factors such as age, gender, family history, and lifestyle habits, leading to omission of some important examination items or excessive examinations.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers provide personalized customization services, tailoring examination plans according to clients' specific situations. Aether Dragon Medical assists clients in communicating with examination experts to develop the most suitable examination plans.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Limited Access to Advanced Detection Technologies

 

Some advanced detection technologies (such as liquid biopsy, multi-omics testing, AI imaging-assisted diagnosis, etc.) are only available in a few research institutions in Belgium, making it difficult for ordinary people to access these cutting-edge detection services.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese health examination centers have introduced multiple international advanced detection technologies, including liquid biopsy for early cancer screening, multi-omics health assessment, AI medical image analysis, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can help clients obtain these cutting-edge detection services.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Post-examination Health Management Services

 

After health examinations in Belgium, there is a lack of systematic health management services. Health examination report interpretations are simple, lacking follow-up health intervention guidance and tracking follow-ups, and the value of examinations is not fully realized.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese health examination centers provide comprehensive post-examination management services, including professional report interpretation, health risk assessment, personalized health intervention plans, regular follow-ups, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist clients in establishing long-term health management files.
