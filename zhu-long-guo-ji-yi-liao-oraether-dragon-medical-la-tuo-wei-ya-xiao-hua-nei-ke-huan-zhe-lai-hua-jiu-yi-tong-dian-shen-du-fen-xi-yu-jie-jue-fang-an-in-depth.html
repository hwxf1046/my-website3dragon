### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：内镜检查等待时间过长

胃肠镜检查等待时间普遍较长，平均需要等待8-12周。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科中心可提供快速内镜检查通道，从预约到检查通常在1周内完成。

 

#### 痛点二：炎症性肠病管理资源不足

克罗恩病、溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病的专科中心数量有限。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院消化内科设有炎症性肠病专科门诊，可开展多种生物制剂治疗。

 

#### 痛点三：早期消化道肿瘤筛查不足

消化道肿瘤筛查项目覆盖有限，许多患者发现时已是中晚期。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科中心广泛开展消化道早癌筛查，配备先进的内镜设备。

 

#### 痛点四：复杂肝胆胰疾病诊疗困难

复杂肝胆胰疾病的确诊和治疗中心有限。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院设有肝胆胰专科门诊，配备先进的影像学和内镜设备。

 

#### 痛点五：功能性胃肠病缺乏有效治疗

缺乏系统性的综合治疗方案，患者症状反复。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科采用中西医结合的综合治疗方案。

As a Baltic nation, Latvia has relatively limited medical resources. In the field of gastroenterology, long waiting times for endoscopic examinations, insufficient resources for inflammatory bowel disease management, and limited coverage of early gastrointestinal cancer screening are becoming major troubles for Latvian gastroenterology patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times for Endoscopic Examinations

Gastrointestinal endoscopy waiting times are generally long, averaging 8-12 weeks.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology centers can provide fast-track endoscopic examination channels, completing the process from appointment to examination usually within 1 week.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Resources for Inflammatory Bowel Disease Management

Limited number of specialty centers for inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese hospital gastroenterology departments have inflammatory bowel disease specialty clinics that can conduct various biologic treatments.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Early Gastrointestinal Cancer Screening

Gastrointestinal cancer screening programs have limited coverage, and many patients are diagnosed at middle or advanced stages.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology centers extensively carry out early gastrointestinal cancer screening, equipped with advanced endoscopic equipment.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Diagnosing and Treating Complex Hepatobiliary and Pancreatic Diseases

Limited centers capable of diagnosing and treating complex hepatobiliary and pancreatic diseases.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have hepatobiliary and pancreatic specialty clinics, equipped with advanced imaging and endoscopic equipment.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Lack of Effective Treatment for Functional Gastrointestinal Disorders

Lack of systematic comprehensive treatment plans, patients experience recurrent symptoms.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology departments adopt integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine comprehensive treatment plans.
