马耳他患者跨境就医需要协调多个国家的医疗系统，语言障碍、保险理赔、病历转移等问题给家庭带来额外负担。

眼科手术后需要长期随访，但马耳他眼科医生短缺，患者难以获得及时的术后复查和调整治疗方案的机会。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Maltese Ophthalmology Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Severe Shortage of Ophthalmology Specialists

 

Malta has a limited number of ophthalmologists nationwide with insufficient subspecialty differentiation. Patients often need to wait weeks to months to see a specialist. For patients with continuously declining vision, this delay may lead to irreversible vision damage.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese ophthalmic centers can provide fast-track medical services, completing the process from initial consultation to treatment usually within 1-2 weeks. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top ophthalmic specialty hospitals such as Beijing Tongren Hospital and Sun Yat-sen University Zhongshan Ophthalmic Center.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Advanced Ophthalmic Equipment

 

Ophthalmic clinic equipment in Malta is relatively basic, lacking some advanced examination and treatment equipment (such as OCT angiography, femtosecond laser, etc.), affecting disease diagnosis and treatment outcomes.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese ophthalmic centers are equipped with the most advanced international ophthalmic equipment, enabling precise diagnosis and minimally invasive treatment. Aether Dragon Medical assists patients in obtaining diagnostic and treatment services supported by advanced equipment.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Capabilities for Complex Eye Disease Diagnosis and Treatment

 

For high-difficulty ophthalmic surgeries such as complex retinal diseases, glaucoma, and corneal transplantation, the number of hospitals capable of performing these procedures in Malta is limited. Patients often need to travel to Italy or other European countries for medical visits.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese ophthalmic centers have huge annual surgical volumes, and doctors have accumulated rich experience in handling complex cases. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with authoritative expert teams.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Cross-border Medical Care Coordination

 

Maltese patients seeking cross-border medical care need to coordinate medical systems across multiple countries. Issues such as language barriers, insurance claims, and medical record transfers bring additional burdens to families.

 

**Solution:** Aether Dragon Medical provides one-stop medical services in China, including full-process support such as visa assistance, translation accompaniment, insurance consultation, etc.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Inconvenient Postoperative Follow-up Management

 

Long-term follow-up is required after ophthalmic surgery, but Malta has a shortage of ophthalmologists, making it difficult for patients to obtain timely postoperative examinations and opportunities to adjust treatment plans.

 

**Solution:** Aether Dragon Medical provides remote follow-up management services, assisting patients in maintaining contact with domestic attending physicians and providing regular follow-up reminders.
