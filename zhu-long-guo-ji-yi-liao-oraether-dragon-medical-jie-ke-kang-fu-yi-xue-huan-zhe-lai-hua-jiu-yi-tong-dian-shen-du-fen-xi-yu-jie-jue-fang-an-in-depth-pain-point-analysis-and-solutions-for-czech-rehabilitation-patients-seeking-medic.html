### 引言

 

捷克拥有完善的医疗体系，但在康复医学领域，康复机构等待时间长、康复治疗师短缺、高端康复设备可及性有限等问题，正成为捷克康复患者面临的主要困扰。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持。

 

### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：康复机构等待时间过长

从转诊到开始康复治疗平均需要等待8-12周。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级康复医学中心可提供快速就诊通道，从评估到开始康复训练通常在1周内完成。

 

#### 痛点二：康复治疗师资源不足

康复治疗师数量有限，且分布不均。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型康复医学中心拥有充足的康复治疗师团队。

 

#### 痛点三：高端康复设备可及性有限

机器人辅助康复系统等先进设备仅在少数中心配备。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级康复中心配备了国际先进的康复设备。

 

#### 痛点四：长期康复费用高昂

长期康复治疗给家庭带来沉重经济负担。

 

**解决方案：** 中国康复医学中心的治疗费用仅为捷克的1/3至1/2。

 

#### 痛点五：居家康复指导不足

患者出院后缺乏系统的居家康复指导。

 

**解决方案：** 中国康复中心提供详细的居家康复指导方案。

  3. **专业康复医学翻译团队**：准确翻译各类功能评估量表、康复计划等
  4. **个性化康复方案制定**：物理治疗、作业治疗、言语治疗等多维度干预
  5. **费用透明合理**：所有费用提前告知，无隐形消费

### Introduction

 

Czech Republic has a comprehensive healthcare system, but in the field of rehabilitation medicine, long waiting times at rehabilitation institutions, shortage of rehabilitation therapists, and limited access to high-end rehabilitation equipment are becoming major troubles for Czech rehabilitation patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times at Rehabilitation Institutions

Average waiting time from referral to starting rehabilitation treatment is 8-12 weeks.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese rehabilitation medicine centers can provide fast-track medical services, completing the process from evaluation to starting rehabilitation training usually within 1 week.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Rehabilitation Therapist Resources

Limited number of rehabilitation therapists with uneven distribution.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese rehabilitation medicine centers have sufficient rehabilitation therapist teams.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Access to High-end Rehabilitation Equipment

Advanced equipment such as robot-assisted rehabilitation systems is only available in a few centers.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese rehabilitation centers are equipped with internationally advanced rehabilitation equipment.

 

#### Pain Point 4: High Costs of Long-term Rehabilitation

Long-term rehabilitation brings heavy financial burden to families.

 

**Solution:** Treatment costs at Chinese rehabilitation medicine centers are only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Czech Republic.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Home Rehabilitation Guidance

Patients lack systematic home rehabilitation guidance after discharge.

 

**Solution:** Chinese rehabilitation centers provide detailed home rehabilitation guidance plans.
