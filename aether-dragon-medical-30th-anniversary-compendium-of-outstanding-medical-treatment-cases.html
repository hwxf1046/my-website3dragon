This criterion is to verify the full coverage of our service capabilities and the whole-process escort for patients. 2. **Difficulty Coefficient of Patient's Medical Treatment** The case must have clear medical treatment barriers that the patient cannot break through by himself/herself, including but not limited to: rare diseases with no clear treatment plan in the patient's home country, difficult and critical diseases with limited treatment options in the patient's home country, language barriers that cannot be solved by the patient himself/herself, information asymmetry leading to mismatch of high-quality medical resources, complex cross-border medical procedures that cannot be completed by the patient himself/herself, and patients with special needs (such as disabled patients, elderly patients over 80 years old, minor patients, pregnant patients) who cannot complete the medical treatment journey independently. The higher the difficulty coefficient, the higher the priority of shortlisting. 3. **Positivity of Medical Treatment Outcome** The case must have a clear positive medical outcome after the patient received treatment in China, including but not limited to: significant improvement of clinical symptoms, significant prolongation of survival period for tumor patients, significant improvement of quality of life, cure of diseases, effective control of chronic disease progression, successful completion of surgical treatment with no serious complications, and good long-term follow-up prognosis. Cases with no clear positive outcome will not be shortlisted. This criterion is to verify that our service has effectively helped patients obtain high-quality medical results. 4. **Irreplaceability of Service Value** The case must clearly reflect the irreplaceable value of Aether Dragon Medical's service in the whole process of patient's medical treatment, that is, the patient cannot complete the high-quality medical treatment journey in China without our professional services. The value includes but is not limited to: precise matching of the most suitable medical experts and hospitals for the patient's condition, avoiding misdiagnosis and mistreatment; professional medical translation to ensure 100% accurate transmission of medical information between doctors and patients, avoiding medical risks caused by information deviation; solving the complex procedures of cross-border medical treatment, saving a lot of time and energy for patients; providing personalized life arrangement services, allowing patients to focus on treatment without distractions; and long-term follow-up docking to ensure the continuity of treatment and rehabilitation. 5. **Industry Benchmarking and Demonstration Significance** The case must have clear benchmarking and demonstration significance for overseas patients of the same type with the same disease, that is, the service model and medical treatment path of this case can be replicated and referenced for patients with the same disease seeking medical treatment in China. For cases of rare diseases, difficult and critical diseases, and special patient groups, the priority of shortlisting is higher, because they can provide more valuable reference for more patients with the same dilemma. 6. **Patient Satisfaction and Word-of-Mouth Recognition** The case must have a full score of patient satisfaction evaluation, and the patient has given clear positive feedback and word-of-mouth recognition. Including but not limited to: the patient's explicit thank-you letter, recommendation to other overseas patients, long-term friendly cooperative relationship with Aether Dragon Medical, and no complaints during the whole service process. This criterion is the most direct verification of our service quality. 7. **Compliance and Standardization of Service Process** The whole service process of the case must strictly abide by the relevant laws and regulations of China and the patient's home country on cross-border medical services, strictly abide by the core positioning of "no diagnosis, no treatment", and all medical diagnosis and treatment behaviors are completed by formal tertiary Class A hospitals and licensed doctors in China. There are no illegal or non-compliant behaviors in the whole service process. This is the bottom line criterion for shortlisting. --- ## 30周年优秀案例评选标准 本案例集收录的所有案例，均严格按照以下7项核心标准进行遴选，只有满足5项及以上核心标准、且无患者投诉不良记录的案例，方可进入候选名单，最终由烛龙国际医疗资深服务顾问、病历翻译专家、跨境医疗服务管理专家组成的30周年案例评审委员会审定入选。具体评选标准如下： 1. **服务全流程闭环完整性** 案例必须覆盖烛龙国际医疗全服务周期，包含行前咨询、病历整理翻译、医院与专家精准匹配、专家预约、签证入境协助、在华医疗翻译陪同、在华生活安排、治疗结束回国后的随访与康复对接全流程。服务环节缺失的案例，不予入围。本标准用于验证我们服务能力的全维度覆盖，以及对患者就医全周期的全程护航。 2. **患者就医难度系数** 案例必须具备患者自身无法突破的明确就医壁垒，包括但不限于：患者所在国家无明确治疗方案的罕见病、患者所在国家治疗手段受限的疑难危重症、患者自身无法解决的语言障碍、信息不对称导致的优质医疗资源错配、患者自身无法完成的复杂跨境就医流程、无法独立完成就医旅程的特殊需求患者（如残障患者、80岁以上高龄患者、未成年患者、妊娠患者等）。就医难度系数越高，入围优先级越高。 3. **诊疗结果正向性** 案例必须具备患者在华接受诊疗后明确的正向医疗结果，包括但不限于：临床症状显著改善、肿瘤患者生存期显著延长、生活质量大幅提升、疾病实现临床治愈、慢性病进展得到有效控制、手术治疗顺利完成且无严重并发症、长期随访预后良好。无明确正向结果的案例，不予入围。本标准用于验证我们的服务，切实帮助患者获得了优质的医疗结局。 4. **服务价值不可替代性** 案例必须清晰体现烛龙国际医疗的服务，在患者就医全流程中的不可替代价值，即没有我们的专业服务，患者无法完成本次高质量的在华就医旅程。该价值包括但不限于：为患者精准匹配了最适配病情的医疗专家与医院，规避了误诊误治风险；专业医疗翻译保障医患之间医疗信息100%精准传递，规避了信息偏差带来的医疗风险；解决了跨境就医的复杂流程问题，为患者节省了大量时间与精力；提供个性化生活安排服务，让患者可以无后顾之忧地专注于治疗；长期随访对接，保障了治疗与康复的连续性。 5. **行业标杆性与示范意义** 案例必须对同病种、同类型的海外患者，具备明确的标杆性与示范意义，即该案例的服务模式与就医路径，可为同病种来华就医的患者提供可复制、可参考的范本。其中罕见病、疑难危重症、特殊患者群体的案例，入围优先级更高，因为它们能为更多有相同困境的患者，提供更具价值的参考。 6. **患者满意度与口碑认可** 案例必须具备满分的患者满意度评价，且患者给出了明确的正向反馈与口碑认可。包括但不限于：患者明确的感谢信、向其他海外患者的推荐、与烛龙国际医疗保持长期友好的合作关系，且全服务流程无任何投诉记录。本标准是对我们服务质量最直接的验证。 7. **服务流程合规性与规范性** 案例的全服务流程，必须严格遵守中国与患者所在国家关于跨境医疗服务的相关法律法规，严格坚守“不诊断、不治疗”的核心定位，所有医疗诊疗行为，均由中国正规三甲医院与执业医师完成，全服务流程无任何违法违规行为。这是案例入围的底线标准。 --- # Chapter 1: Outstanding Cases of Nervous System Diseases (40 Types) ## 1.
