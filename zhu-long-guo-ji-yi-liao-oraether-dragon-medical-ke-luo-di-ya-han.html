## 中文版

 

### 引言

 

克罗地亚拥有完善的医疗体系，但在罕见病诊疗领域，专科医生短缺、诊断周期长、治疗药物可及性有限以及部分罕见病缺乏有效治疗方案等问题，正成为克罗地亚罕见病患者面临的主要挑战。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持。

 

### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：罕见病诊断周期过长

罕见病诊断平均需要2-4年，患者往往需要辗转多个科室和医院才能获得确诊。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院设有罕见病多学科诊疗中心，配备先进的基因检测设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，将诊断时间缩短至数周。

 

#### 痛点二：专科医生资源极度稀缺

罕见病专科医生数量有限，且主要集中在萨格勒布等大城市。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型罕见病诊疗中心汇聚了国内最顶尖的罕见病专家。

 

#### 痛点三：治疗药物可及性有限

许多罕见病特效药在克罗地亚尚未获批上市，或价格极其昂贵。

 

**解决方案：** 中国近年来加快罕见病药物审批速度，多家医院参与国际多中心临床试验。

 

#### 痛点四：多学科协作诊疗困难

罕见病往往涉及多个系统，需要多学科协作诊疗，但协调困难。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型罕见病中心实行 MDT 多学科会诊制度。

 

#### 痛点五：长期随访管理不足

部分地区缺乏完善的罕见病随访体系。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级罕见病中心提供长期随访管理服务。

### Introduction

 

Croatia has a comprehensive healthcare system, but in the field of rare disease diagnosis and treatment, shortage of specialist doctors, long diagnostic cycles, limited access to treatment drugs, and lack of effective treatment plans for some rare diseases are becoming major challenges for Croatian rare disease patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessively Long Rare Disease Diagnostic Cycles

Rare disease diagnosis takes an average of 2-4 years. Patients often need to visit multiple departments and hospitals before obtaining a confirmed diagnosis.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have established rare disease multidisciplinary diagnosis and treatment centers, equipped with advanced genetic testing equipment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations, shortening diagnostic time to several weeks.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Extremely Scarce Specialist Doctor Resources

Limited number of rare disease specialist doctors, mainly concentrated in big cities like Zagreb.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese rare disease diagnosis and treatment centers gather the top rare disease experts in China.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Access to Treatment Drugs

Many rare disease-specific drugs have not yet been approved for marketing in Croatia, or prices are extremely expensive.

 

**Solution:** China has accelerated rare disease drug approval in recent years, with multiple hospitals participating in international multi-center clinical trials.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Multidisciplinary Collaborative Diagnosis and Treatment

Rare diseases often involve multiple systems and require multidisciplinary collaborative diagnosis and treatment, but coordination is difficult.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese rare disease centers implement MDT consultation systems.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Long-term Follow-up Management

Some areas lack complete rare disease follow-up systems.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese rare disease centers provide long-term follow-up management services.
