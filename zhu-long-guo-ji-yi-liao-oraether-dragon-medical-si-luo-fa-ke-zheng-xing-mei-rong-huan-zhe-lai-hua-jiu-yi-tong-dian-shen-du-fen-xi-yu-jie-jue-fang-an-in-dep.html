### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：公立医疗覆盖范围有限

美容整形手术基本不在医保覆盖范围内，患者需要完全自费。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院整形外科提供性价比高的整形美容服务，费用仅为斯洛伐克的1/3至1/2。

 

#### 痛点二：私立诊所费用极其高昂

隆胸手术费用可达5000-7000欧元，面部拉皮手术费用更高。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶尖整形外科中心的手术费用仅为斯洛伐克的1/3至1/2。

 

#### 痛点三：等待时间过长

知名整形医生的预约需要等待数周至数月。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型整形外科中心可快速安排手术，从咨询到手术通常在2-4周内完成。

 

#### 痛点四：复杂整形手术经验不足

多次修复手术、高难度鼻整形等复杂手术能够开展的医生有限。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级整形外科中心年手术量巨大，医生积累了丰富的复杂病例处理经验。

 

#### 痛点五：术后随访管理不便

部分地区整形外科医生短缺，患者难以获得及时的术后复查。

 

**解决方案：** 烛龙医疗提供远程随访管理服务。

### Introduction

 

Slovakia has a comprehensive healthcare system, but in the field of plastic surgery, limited public healthcare coverage, extremely high costs of private clinics, long waiting times, and limited accessibility of some complex plastic surgery techniques are becoming major troubles for Slovak plastic surgery patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Limited Public Healthcare Coverage

Cosmetic plastic surgeries are basically not covered by health insurance, requiring patients to pay entirely out-of-pocket.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese hospital plastic surgery departments provide cost-effective plastic surgery services, with costs only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Slovakia.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Extremely High Private Clinic Costs

Breast augmentation surgery can cost €5,000-7,000, and facelift surgery costs are even higher.

 

**Solution:** Surgical costs at top Chinese plastic surgery centers are only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Slovakia.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Excessive Waiting Times

Appointments with well-known plastic surgeons require waiting several weeks to months.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese plastic surgery centers can quickly arrange surgeries, completing the process from consultation to surgery usually within 2-4 weeks.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Insufficient Experience in Complex Plastic Surgeries

Limited number of doctors capable of performing complex surgeries such as multiple revision surgeries and high-difficulty rhinoplasty.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese plastic surgery centers have huge annual surgical volumes, and doctors have accumulated rich experience in handling complex cases.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Inconvenient Postoperative Follow-up Management

Shortage of plastic surgeons in some areas makes it difficult for patients to obtain timely postoperative examinations.

 

**Solution:** Aether Dragon Medical provides remote follow-up management services.
