### 西班牙内分泌科患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：专科医生短缺导致等待时间长

 

根据西班牙内分泌与营养学会数据，西班牙每10万人口仅有约6名内分泌科医生，远低于实际需求。糖尿病患者从转诊到获得专科医生面诊平均需要等待10-14周，对于血糖控制不佳的患者，这种延误可能导致严重并发症。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型三甲医院内分泌科医生资源相对充足，可快速安排专家面诊。烛龙医疗与北京协和医院内分泌科、上海瑞金医院内分泌代谢病科等国内顶尖内分泌科合作，确保患者在1周内获得权威专家诊疗。

 

#### 痛点二：糖尿病并发症筛查不足

 

西班牙公共医疗体系下，糖尿病患者的并发症筛查资源有限，眼底检查、神经病变评估、肾功能监测等往往需要长时间等待，导致并发症发现晚、治疗效果差。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级医院内分泌科提供完善的糖尿病并发症筛查服务，可在短时间内完成全面评估。烛龙医疗协助患者快速完成各项检查，尽早发现并干预并发症。

 

#### 痛点三：罕见内分泌疾病诊疗困难

 

对于垂体瘤、肾上腺疾病、甲状旁腺功能异常等罕见内分泌疾病，西班牙能够确诊和治疗的中心有限，患者往往需要跨区域就医，诊断周期长且准确性难以保证。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院内分泌科设有罕见病专科门诊，配备先进的激素检测和影像学检查设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，缩短诊断时间，尽早开始针对性治疗。

 

#### 痛点四：肥胖症手术治疗等待时间长

 

对于重度肥胖症患者，西班牙公立医疗系统等待减重手术的时间普遍较长，平均等待时间超过12个月。长期等待不仅影响治疗效果，还可能加重相关并发症。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院减重代谢外科可提供快速手术通道，从评估到手术通常在4-8周内完成。烛龙医疗与多家减重中心合作，确保患者及时获得手术治疗。

 

#### 痛点五：慢性病长期管理不足

 

对于糖尿病、甲状腺疾病等慢性内分泌疾病，西班牙缺乏系统性的长期管理方案，患者出院后缺乏持续随访和指导，导致病情控制不佳、反复住院。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级内分泌科提供慢性病患者长期管理服务，包括定期随访、用药调整、生活方式指导等。烛龙医疗可协助建立患者与国内医生的长期联系，确保持续管理。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Spanish Endocrinology Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Long Waiting Times Due to Specialist Shortage

 

According to the Spanish Society of Endocrinology and Nutrition, Spain has only about 6 endocrinologists per 100,000 population, far below actual demand. Diabetic patients need to wait an average of 10-14 weeks from referral to seeing a specialist. For patients with poor blood sugar control, this delay may lead to serious complications.

 

**Solution:** Endocrinologist resources in large Chinese tertiary hospitals are relatively sufficient, and specialist consultations can be arranged quickly. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top endocrinology departments such as Peking Union Medical College Hospital Endocrinology Department and Shanghai Ruijin Hospital Endocrinology and Metabolism Department to ensure patients receive authoritative expert diagnosis and treatment within 1 week.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Diabetes Complication Screening

 

Under Spain's public healthcare system, complication screening resources for diabetic patients are limited. Fundus examinations, neuropathy assessments, kidney function monitoring, etc., often require long waiting times, leading to late detection of complications and poor treatment outcomes.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese hospital endocrinology departments provide comprehensive diabetes complication screening services that can complete comprehensive evaluations in a short time. Aether Dragon Medical assists patients in quickly completing various examinations to detect and intervene in complications as early as possible.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Difficulty in Diagnosing and Treating Rare Endocrine Diseases

 

For rare endocrine diseases such as pituitary tumors, adrenal diseases, and parathyroid dysfunction, the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in Spain is limited. Patients often need to seek medical care across regions, with long diagnostic cycles and difficulty ensuring accuracy.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have rare disease specialty clinics in their endocrinology departments, equipped with advanced hormone testing and imaging examination equipment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations, shortening diagnostic time, and starting targeted treatment as early as possible.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Long Waiting Times for Obesity Surgery

 

For severely obese patients, waiting times for bariatric surgery in Spain's public healthcare system are generally long, with average waiting times exceeding 12 months. Long-term waiting not only affects treatment outcomes but may also worsen related complications.

 

**Solution:** Bariatric and metabolic surgery departments in large Chinese hospitals can provide fast-track surgical channels, completing the process from evaluation to surgery usually within 4-8 weeks. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with multiple bariatric centers to ensure patients receive timely surgical treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Long-term Management of Chronic Diseases

 

For chronic endocrine diseases such as diabetes and thyroid diseases, Spain lacks systematic long-term management plans. Patients lack continuous follow-up and guidance after discharge, leading to poor disease control and repeated hospitalizations.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese endocrinology departments provide long-term management services for chronic disease patients, including regular follow-ups, medication adjustments, lifestyle guidance, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in establishing long-term connections between patients and domestic doctors to ensure continuous management.
