### 新西兰肾内科患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：肾移植等待时间过长

 

新西兰肾源极度稀缺，肾移植等待时间平均长达3-5年。许多患者在等待期间病情恶化，甚至失去移植机会。对于终末期肾病患者，这种等待可能是致命的。

 

**解决方案：** 中国肾移植中心数量众多，手术经验丰富。虽然外国患者在中国进行肾移植需要符合严格条件，但烛龙医疗可协助评估患者是否符合相关条件，并提供专业的咨询服务。

 

#### 痛点二：透析资源紧张

 

新西兰透析中心数量有限，尤其在南岛和偏远地区，透析资源更加紧张。患者需要长途跋涉至大城市进行透析，或在家进行腹膜透析但缺乏足够支持。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院肾内科配备充足的透析设备和专业人员，可提供血液透析和腹膜透析服务。烛龙医疗可协助需要短期透析支持的患者获得及时治疗。

 

#### 痛点三：慢性肾病管理不足

 

对于慢性肾病早期患者，新西兰缺乏系统性的长期管理方案，患者出院后缺乏持续随访和指导，导致病情进展加快，过早进入透析阶段。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级肾内科中心提供慢性肾病长期管理服务，包括定期随访、用药调整、饮食指导、并发症预防等。烛龙医疗可协助建立患者与国内医生的长期联系。

 

#### 痛点四：罕见肾病诊疗困难

 

对于IgA肾病、狼疮性肾炎、ANCA相关性血管炎肾损害等复杂肾病，新西兰能够确诊和治疗的中心有限，患者往往需要跨区域就医。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院肾内科设有疑难肾病专科门诊，配备先进的病理检测和免疫学检测设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，缩短诊断时间。

 

#### 痛点五：肾病营养指导不足

 

肾病患者需要严格的饮食管理，但新西兰肾病营养指导资源有限，患者难以获得个性化的饮食方案，影响治疗效果和生活质量。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型肾内科中心配备专业营养师，可为患者制定个性化的饮食方案，包括蛋白质、磷、钾等营养素的精确控制。烛龙医疗协助患者获得专业的营养指导。

### Introduction

### Core Pain Point Analysis for New Zealand Nephrology Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessively Long Kidney Transplant Waiting Times

 

Kidney sources in New Zealand are extremely scarce, with kidney transplant waiting times averaging 3-5 years. Many patients' conditions deteriorate during the waiting period, even losing transplant opportunities. For end-stage renal disease patients, this waiting can be fatal.

 

**Solution:** China has numerous kidney transplant centers with rich surgical experience. Although foreign patients undergoing kidney transplantation in China need to meet strict conditions, Aether Dragon Medical can assist in evaluating whether patients meet relevant conditions and provide professional consultation services.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Tight Dialysis Resources

 

The number of dialysis centers in New Zealand is limited, especially in the South Island and remote areas, where dialysis resources are even tighter. Patients need to travel long distances to big cities for dialysis, or perform peritoneal dialysis at home but lack sufficient support.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese hospital nephrology departments are equipped with sufficient dialysis equipment and professional personnel, providing hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis services. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients needing short-term dialysis support in obtaining timely treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Chronic Kidney Disease Management

 

For early-stage chronic kidney disease patients, New Zealand lacks systematic long-term management plans. Patients lack continuous follow-up and guidance after discharge, leading to accelerated disease progression and premature entry into the dialysis stage.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese nephrology centers provide long-term management services for chronic kidney diseases, including regular follow-ups, medication adjustments, dietary guidance, complication prevention, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in establishing long-term connections between patients and domestic doctors.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Diagnosing and Treating Rare Kidney Diseases

 

For complex kidney diseases such as IgA nephropathy, lupus nephritis, and ANCA-associated vasculitis kidney damage, the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in New Zealand is limited, and patients often need to seek medical care across regions.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have difficult kidney disease specialty clinics in their nephrology departments, equipped with advanced pathological testing and immunological detection equipment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations to shorten diagnostic time.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Kidney Disease Nutritional Guidance

 

Kidney disease patients require strict dietary management, but kidney disease nutritional guidance resources in New Zealand are limited, making it difficult for patients to obtain personalized dietary plans, affecting treatment outcomes and quality of life.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese nephrology centers are equipped with professional nutritionists who can develop personalized dietary plans for patients, including precise control of nutrients such as protein, phosphorus, and potassium. Aether Dragon Medical assists patients in obtaining professional nutritional guidance.
