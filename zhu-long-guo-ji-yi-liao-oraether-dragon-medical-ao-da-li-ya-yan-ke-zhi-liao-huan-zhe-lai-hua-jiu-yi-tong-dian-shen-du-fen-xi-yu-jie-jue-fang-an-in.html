### 引言

### 澳大利亚眼科患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：公立医疗等待时间过长

 

澳大利亚公立医疗系统下，非紧急眼科手术等待时间普遍较长。根据澳大利亚眼科医学院数据，白内障手术平均等待时间为3-4个月，玻璃体视网膜手术等待时间甚至长达6个月以上。对于视力持续下降的患者，这种等待可能导致不可逆的视力损害。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级眼科中心可提供快速就诊通道，从初诊到手术通常在1-2周内完成。烛龙医疗与北京同仁医院、中山大学中山眼科中心等国内顶尖眼科专科医院合作，确保患者及时获得治疗。

 

#### 痛点二：私立医疗费用高昂

 

选择私立医院可缩短等待时间，但澳大利亚私立眼科手术费用极高。一次白内障手术费用可达3000-5000澳元，复杂视网膜手术费用更高，即使有私人保险覆盖，自付部分仍给家庭带来负担。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶尖眼科医院的手术费用仅为澳大利亚的1/3至1/2，且医疗质量达到国际先进水平。烛龙医疗帮助患者在保证治疗效果的同时大幅降低经济负担。

 

#### 痛点三：偏远地区医疗资源匮乏

 

澳大利亚地广人稀，偏远地区和农村的眼科医疗资源严重不足。当地居民往往需要长途跋涉至大城市就诊，交通和住宿成本高昂，且延误治疗时机。

 

**解决方案：** 烛龙医疗可为澳大利亚偏远地区患者提供一站式来华就医服务，包括签证协助、机票预订、接机入住等全流程安排，让患者无需在国内奔波即可获得优质医疗服务。

 

#### 痛点四：先进治疗技术可及性有限

 

部分眼科先进治疗技术（如基因治疗遗传性视网膜病变、新型青光眼微创手术等）在澳大利亚仅在少数中心开展，且准入标准严格，许多患者无法及时获得这些前沿治疗。

 

**解决方案：** 中国在眼科先进治疗领域发展迅速，多家医院已开展基因治疗临床试验、微创青光眼手术等国际前沿技术。烛龙医疗可帮助患者评估是否符合治疗条件，并协助对接相关医院。

 

#### 痛点五：术后随访管理不便

 

眼科手术后需要长期随访，但澳大利亚部分地区眼科医生短缺，患者难以获得及时的术后复查和调整治疗方案的机会，影响治疗效果。

 

**解决方案：** 烛龙医疗提供远程随访管理服务，协助患者与国内主治医生保持联系，定期复查提醒，并可协助安排必要的复查检查，确保治疗效果持续优化。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Australian Ophthalmology Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times in Public Healthcare

 

Under Australia's public healthcare system, waiting times for non-urgent ophthalmic surgeries are generally long. According to the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Ophthalmologists, the average waiting time for cataract surgery is 3-4 months, and vitreoretinal surgery waiting time can even exceed 6 months. For patients with continuously declining vision, this waiting may lead to irreversible vision damage.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese ophthalmic centers can provide fast-track medical services, completing the process from initial consultation to surgery usually within 1-2 weeks. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top ophthalmic specialty hospitals such as Beijing Tongren Hospital and Sun Yat-sen University Zhongshan Ophthalmic Center to ensure patients receive timely treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 2: High Private Medical Costs

 

Choosing private hospitals can shorten waiting times, but private ophthalmic surgery costs in Australia are extremely high. A cataract surgery can cost AUD 3,000-5,000, and complex retinal surgery costs are even higher. Even with private insurance coverage, out-of-pocket expenses still bring burden to families.

 

**Solution:** Surgical costs at top Chinese ophthalmic hospitals are only 1/3 to 1/2 of those in Australia, while medical quality has reached international advanced levels. Aether Dragon Medical helps patients significantly reduce financial burden while ensuring treatment effectiveness.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Scarcity of Medical Resources in Remote Areas

 

Australia has a vast territory with sparse population, and ophthalmic medical resources in remote areas and rural regions are severely insufficient. Local residents often need to travel long distances to big cities for medical visits, with high transportation and accommodation costs, and delayed treatment timing.

 

**Solution:** Aether Dragon Medical can provide one-stop medical services in China for patients in remote Australian areas, including full-process arrangements such as visa assistance, flight booking, airport pickup and check-in, allowing patients to obtain quality medical services without rushing domestically.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Limited Access to Advanced Treatment Technologies

 

Some advanced ophthalmic treatment technologies (such as gene therapy for hereditary retinal diseases, new minimally invasive glaucoma surgeries, etc.) are only available in a few centers in Australia with strict access criteria, preventing many patients from obtaining these cutting-edge treatments promptly.

 

**Solution:** China has developed rapidly in the field of advanced ophthalmic treatments, with multiple hospitals already conducting international frontier technologies such as gene therapy clinical trials and minimally invasive glaucoma surgeries. Aether Dragon Medical can help patients evaluate whether they meet treatment conditions and assist in connecting with relevant hospitals.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Inconvenient Postoperative Follow-up Management

 

Long-term follow-up is required after ophthalmic surgery, but some areas in Australia have a shortage of ophthalmologists, making it difficult for patients to obtain timely postoperative examinations and opportunities to adjust treatment plans, affecting treatment outcomes.

 

**Solution:** Aether Dragon Medical provides remote follow-up management services, assisting patients in maintaining contact with domestic attending physicians, providing regular follow-up reminders, and assisting in arranging necessary follow-up examinations to ensure continuous optimization of treatment effects.
