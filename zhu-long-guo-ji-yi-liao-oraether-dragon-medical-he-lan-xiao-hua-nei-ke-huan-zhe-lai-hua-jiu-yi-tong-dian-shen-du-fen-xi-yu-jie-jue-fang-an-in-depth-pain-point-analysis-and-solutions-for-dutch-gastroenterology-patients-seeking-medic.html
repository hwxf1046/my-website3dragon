### 荷兰消化内科患者的核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：内镜检查等待时间过长

 

荷兰公共医疗体系下，胃肠镜检查等待时间普遍较长。根据荷兰消化病学学会数据，非紧急胃镜检查平均等待时间为8-10周，结肠镜检查等待时间甚至长达12周以上。对于有消化道症状的患者，这种延误可能影响早期诊断和治疗。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科中心可提供快速内镜检查通道，从预约到检查通常在1周内完成。烛龙医疗与北京友谊医院消化内科、上海仁济医院消化内科等国内顶尖消化科合作，确保患者及时获得检查。

 

#### 痛点二：炎症性肠病管理资源不足

 

对于克罗恩病、溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病，荷兰虽然有一定的诊疗能力，但专科中心数量有限，生物制剂等先进治疗药物使用受限，部分患者治疗效果不佳。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院消化内科设有炎症性肠病专科门诊，可开展多种生物制剂治疗和个体化治疗方案。烛龙医疗可协助患者对接专业IBD中心，获得更有效的治疗。

 

#### 痛点三：早期消化道肿瘤筛查不足

 

荷兰消化道肿瘤筛查项目覆盖有限，许多患者发现时已是中晚期，错过最佳治疗时机。内镜下早癌筛查和微创治疗技术普及度不高。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科中心广泛开展消化道早癌筛查，配备先进的内镜设备和经验丰富的医生团队。烛龙医疗可协助患者进行全面的消化道检查，尽早发现并治疗早期病变。

 

#### 痛点四：复杂肝胆胰疾病诊疗困难

 

对于复杂肝胆胰疾病（如自身免疫性肝病、胰腺囊性肿瘤等），荷兰能够确诊和治疗的中心有限，患者往往需要跨区域就医，诊断周期长且准确性难以保证。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院消化内科或肝胆外科设有肝胆胰专科门诊，配备先进的影像学和内镜设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，缩短诊断时间。

 

#### 痛点五：功能性胃肠病缺乏有效治疗

 

对于肠易激综合征、功能性消化不良等功能性胃肠病，荷兰多以对症治疗为主，缺乏系统性的综合治疗方案，患者症状反复、生活质量受影响。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级消化内科采用中西医结合的综合治疗方案，包括药物治疗、心理干预、饮食调理等多维度措施。烛龙医疗可协助患者获得更全面的治疗方案。

### Introduction

 

The Netherlands has a high-quality healthcare service system, but in the field of gastroenterology, shortage of specialist doctors, long waiting times for endoscopic examinations, insufficient resources for managing complex diseases such as inflammatory bowel disease, and limited access to some advanced treatment technologies are becoming major challenges for Dutch gastroenterology patients. ### Core Pain Point Analysis for Dutch Gastroenterology Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times for Endoscopic Examinations

 

Under the Dutch public healthcare system, waiting times for gastrointestinal endoscopy are generally long. According to the Dutch Society of Gastroenterology, the average waiting time for non-urgent gastroscopy is 8-10 weeks, and colonoscopy waiting time can even exceed 12 weeks. For patients with gastrointestinal symptoms, this delay may affect early diagnosis and treatment.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology centers can provide fast-track endoscopic examination channels, completing the process from appointment to examination usually within 1 week. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top gastroenterology departments such as Beijing Friendship Hospital Gastroenterology Department and Shanghai Renji Hospital Gastroenterology Department to ensure patients receive timely examinations.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Resources for Inflammatory Bowel Disease Management

 

For inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis, although the Netherlands has certain diagnostic and treatment capabilities, the number of specialty centers is limited, and the use of advanced treatment drugs such as biologics is restricted, resulting in poor treatment outcomes for some patients.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese hospital gastroenterology departments have inflammatory bowel disease specialty clinics that can conduct various biologic treatments and personalized treatment plans. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in connecting with professional IBD centers to obtain more effective treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Insufficient Early Gastrointestinal Cancer Screening

 

The Netherlands has limited coverage of gastrointestinal cancer screening programs, and many patients are diagnosed at middle or advanced stages, missing the optimal treatment timing. The popularity of early cancer screening and minimally invasive treatment techniques via endoscopy is not high.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology centers extensively carry out early gastrointestinal cancer screening, equipped with advanced endoscopic equipment and experienced doctor teams. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in conducting comprehensive gastrointestinal examinations to detect and treat early lesions as soon as possible.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Diagnosing and Treating Complex Hepatobiliary and Pancreatic Diseases

 

For complex hepatobiliary and pancreatic diseases (such as autoimmune liver diseases, pancreatic cystic tumors, etc.), the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in the Netherlands is limited. Patients often need to seek medical care across regions, with long diagnostic cycles and difficulty ensuring accuracy.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have hepatobiliary and pancreatic specialty clinics in their gastroenterology or hepatobiliary surgery departments, equipped with advanced imaging and endoscopic equipment. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations to shorten diagnostic time.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Lack of Effective Treatment for Functional Gastrointestinal Disorders

 

For functional gastrointestinal disorders such as irritable bowel syndrome and functional dyspepsia, the Netherlands mainly focuses on symptomatic treatment, lacking systematic comprehensive treatment plans. Patients experience recurrent symptoms and affected quality of life.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese gastroenterology departments adopt integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine comprehensive treatment plans, including multi-dimensional measures such as drug therapy, psychological intervention, and dietary adjustments. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in obtaining more comprehensive treatment plans.
