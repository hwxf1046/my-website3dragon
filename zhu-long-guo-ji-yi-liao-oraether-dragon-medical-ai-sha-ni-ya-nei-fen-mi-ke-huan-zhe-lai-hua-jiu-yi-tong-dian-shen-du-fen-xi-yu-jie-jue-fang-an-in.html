### 引言

 

爱沙尼亚作为波罗的海国家，医疗资源相对有限。在内分泌科领域，专科医生短缺、等待时间长、罕见内分泌疾病诊疗能力不足等问题，正成为爱沙尼亚内分泌患者面临的主要困扰。烛龙国际医疗（Aether Dragon Medical）专注为海外患者提供来华就医的全流程支持。

 

### 核心痛点分析

 

#### 痛点一：内分泌专科医生短缺

爱沙尼亚内分泌科医生数量有限，患者从转诊到获得专科医生面诊平均需要等待8-12周。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型三甲医院内分泌科医生资源充足，可快速安排专家面诊。

 

#### 痛点二：糖尿病并发症筛查不足

糖尿病患者的并发症筛查资源有限，往往需要长时间等待。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级医院内分泌科提供完善的糖尿病并发症筛查服务。

 

#### 痛点三：罕见内分泌疾病诊疗困难

对于垂体瘤、肾上腺疾病等罕见内分泌疾病，爱沙尼亚能够确诊和治疗的中心有限。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院内分泌科设有罕见病专科门诊。

 

#### 痛点四：肥胖症手术治疗等待时间长

重度肥胖症患者等待减重手术的时间普遍较长。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型医院减重代谢外科可提供快速手术通道。

 

#### 痛点五：慢性病长期管理不足

缺乏系统性的长期管理方案，患者出院后缺乏持续随访。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级内分泌科提供慢性病患者长期管理服务。

### Introduction

 

As a Baltic nation, Estonia has relatively limited medical resources. In the field of endocrinology, shortage of specialist doctors, long waiting times, and insufficient capabilities for rare endocrine disease diagnosis and treatment are becoming major troubles for Estonian endocrinology patients. Aether Dragon Medical specializes in providing end-to-end support for overseas patients seeking medical care in China.

 

### Core Pain Point Analysis

 

#### Pain Point 1: Shortage of Endocrinology Specialists

Estonia has a limited number of endocrinologists, with average waiting time from referral to seeing a specialist being 8-12 weeks.

 

**Solution:** Endocrinologist resources in large Chinese tertiary hospitals are sufficient, and specialist consultations can be arranged quickly.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Diabetes Complication Screening

Complication screening resources for diabetic patients are limited, often requiring long waiting times.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese hospital endocrinology departments provide comprehensive diabetes complication screening services.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Difficulty in Rare Endocrine Disease Diagnosis and Treatment

For rare endocrine diseases such as pituitary tumors and adrenal diseases, the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in Estonia is limited.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have rare disease specialty clinics in their endocrinology departments.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Long Waiting Times for Obesity Surgery

Waiting times for bariatric surgery for severely obese patients are generally long.

 

**Solution:** Bariatric and metabolic surgery departments in large Chinese hospitals can provide fast-track surgical channels.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Long-term Management of Chronic Diseases

Lack of systematic long-term management plans, patients lack continuous follow-up after discharge.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese endocrinology departments provide long-term management services for chronic disease patients.
