第二部分：我们能为你做什么（核心业务）

中文模块

1. 来华就医一站式咨询
病情适合哪家医院？治疗要多久？费用多少？签证怎么办理？——我们一次性帮你理清。

2. 医院&专家精准匹配
骨科、肿瘤、心脑血管、微创、康复、中医……根据你的病情，精准推荐公立三甲、国际部或私立医院，避免乱找、被骗、找不到对口专家。

3. 就医翻译（老外最大痛点）
门诊陪同、检查沟通、手术翻译、病历报告翻译、24小时紧急支持。让语言不再成为治疗的障碍。

4. 就医全程陪同（高客单服务）
从接机、住宿、挂号、排队、缴费、取药，到检查、住院、手术、术后复查，全程有人陪、有人管。

5. 预约代办（老外自己完全搞不定）
专家号加急、住院床位协调、检查项目提前预约、国际患者绿色通道——你只需安心看病，其余交给我们。

6. 病历整理&跨境转诊
国外病历翻译整理，提前传送中国医生评估，出具接诊意见，协助正式转诊。

7. 生活&后勤保障
医院附近住宿、清真/素食/欧美饮食习惯协调、交通接送、生活翻译，让你安心治病，不为琐事分心。

8. 签证&入境辅助
医疗签证材料指导、邀请函协助、入境流程说明，从落地前就开始照顾你。

English Modules

1. One-Stop Medical Consultation
Which hospital is best for your condition? How long will treatment take? What’s the cost? How do I get a visa? — We provide clear answers to all your questions before you arrive.

2. Hospital & Specialist Matching
Orthopedics, oncology, cardiology, minimally invasive surgery, rehabilitation, traditional Chinese medicine — we match you with the right specialist and hospital (public tertiary, international, or private), ensuring you avoid scams and misdirection.

3. Medical Interpretation (The Biggest Pain Point)
Clinic accompaniment, exam and surgery interpretation, medical record and prescription translation, 24/7 emergency support. We remove the language barrier from your healthcare journey.

4. Full-Service Patient Accompaniment (Premium Service)
From airport pickup, accommodation, registration, payment, and pharmacy pickup, to tests, hospitalization, surgery, and post-op follow-ups — we stay with you every step of the way.

5. Appointment & Coordination Services
Expedited specialist appointments, hospital bed coordination, advance scheduling for tests, and an international patient green channel — you focus on your health, and we handle the logistics.

6. Medical Records & Cross-Border Referral
We translate and organize your medical records, share them with Chinese doctors for preliminary assessment, and assist with the official referral process.

7. Logistics & Daily Support
We arrange nearby accommodations, coordinate meals (halal, vegetarian, or Western diets), provide transportation, and offer daily life interpretation.

8. Visa & Arrival Assistance
We guide you through medical visa requirements, help with invitation letters, and explain entry procedures — so you’re supported even before you land.
