#### 痛点一：呼吸专科等待时间过长

 

爱尔兰公共医疗体系下，呼吸科门诊和检查等待时间普遍较长。根据爱尔兰健康服务执行局数据，从转诊到获得呼吸科专家面诊平均需要等待10-14周，肺功能检查等待时间甚至更长。对于呼吸困难的患者，这种延误可能加重病情。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级呼吸科中心可提供快速就诊通道，从初诊到完成检查通常在1周内完成。烛龙医疗与广州医科大学附属第一医院呼吸科、北京朝阳医院呼吸科等国内顶尖呼吸科合作，确保患者及时获得诊疗。

 

#### 痛点二：慢性呼吸系统疾病管理不足

 

对于慢阻肺（COPD）、哮喘、支气管扩张等慢性呼吸系统疾病，爱尔兰缺乏系统性的长期管理方案，患者出院后缺乏持续随访和指导，导致病情反复、频繁住院。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型呼吸科中心提供慢性呼吸系统疾病长期管理服务，包括定期随访、用药指导、肺康复训练、急性加重预防等。烛龙医疗可协助建立患者与国内医生的长期联系。

 

#### 痛点三：肺癌早期筛查覆盖有限

 

爱尔兰肺癌筛查项目覆盖有限，许多患者发现时已是中晚期，错过最佳治疗时机。低剂量CT筛查普及度不高，高危人群筛查率较低。

 

**解决方案：** 中国顶级呼吸科中心广泛开展肺癌早筛项目，配备先进的影像设备和经验丰富的医生团队。烛龙医疗可协助高危人群进行全面的肺部检查，尽早发现并治疗早期病变。

 

#### 痛点四：间质性肺病诊疗困难

 

对于特发性肺纤维化、结节病等间质性肺病，爱尔兰能够确诊和治疗的中心有限，患者往往需要跨区域就医，诊断周期长且准确性难以保证。

 

**解决方案：** 中国多家三甲医院呼吸科设有间质性肺病专科门诊，配备高分辨率CT、肺功能检测、支气管镜等先进设备。烛龙医疗可协助患者快速完成全面检查，缩短诊断时间。

 

#### 痛点五：肺康复治疗资源不足

 

慢性呼吸系统疾病患者需要肺康复治疗，但爱尔兰肺康复项目覆盖有限，许多患者无法获得系统的康复训练，影响生活质量和预后。

 

**解决方案：** 中国大型呼吸科中心提供完善的肺康复服务，包括运动训练、呼吸肌锻炼、营养指导、心理支持等多维度干预。烛龙医疗可协助患者获得系统的肺康复治疗。

### Core Pain Point Analysis for Irish Respiratory Medicine Patients

 

#### Pain Point 1: Excessive Waiting Times for Respiratory Specialists

 

Under Ireland's public healthcare system, waiting times for respiratory outpatient visits and examinations are generally long. According to the Health Service Executive of Ireland, the average waiting time from referral to seeing a respiratory specialist is 10-14 weeks, and pulmonary function test waiting times are even longer. For patients with breathing difficulties, this delay may worsen their condition.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese respiratory centers can provide fast-track medical services, completing the process from initial consultation to completing examinations usually within 1 week. Aether Dragon Medical cooperates with domestic top respiratory departments such as The First Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical University Respiratory Department and Beijing Chaoyang Hospital Respiratory Department to ensure patients receive timely diagnosis and treatment.

 

#### Pain Point 2: Insufficient Management of Chronic Respiratory Diseases

 

For chronic respiratory diseases such as COPD, asthma, and bronchiectasis, Ireland lacks systematic long-term management plans. Patients lack continuous follow-up and guidance after discharge, leading to disease recurrence and frequent hospitalizations.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese respiratory centers provide long-term management services for chronic respiratory diseases, including regular follow-ups, medication guidance, pulmonary rehabilitation training, acute exacerbation prevention, etc. Aether Dragon Medical can assist in establishing long-term connections between patients and domestic doctors.

 

#### Pain Point 3: Limited Coverage of Early Lung Cancer Screening

 

Ireland's lung cancer screening programs have limited coverage, and many patients are diagnosed at middle or advanced stages, missing the optimal treatment timing. Low-dose CT screening popularity is not high, and screening rates for high-risk populations are low.

 

**Solution:** Top Chinese respiratory centers extensively carry out early lung cancer screening programs, equipped with advanced imaging equipment and experienced doctor teams. Aether Dragon Medical can assist high-risk populations in conducting comprehensive lung examinations to detect and treat early lesions as soon as possible.

 

#### Pain Point 4: Difficulty in Diagnosing and Treating Interstitial Lung Diseases

 

For interstitial lung diseases such as idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis and sarcoidosis, the number of centers capable of diagnosis and treatment in Ireland is limited. Patients often need to seek medical care across regions, with long diagnostic cycles and difficulty ensuring accuracy.

 

**Solution:** Multiple Chinese tertiary hospitals have interstitial lung disease specialty clinics in their respiratory departments, equipped with advanced equipment such as high-resolution CT, pulmonary function testing, and bronchoscopy. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in quickly completing comprehensive examinations to shorten diagnostic time.

 

#### Pain Point 5: Insufficient Pulmonary Rehabilitation Resources

 

Patients with chronic respiratory diseases need pulmonary rehabilitation, but pulmonary rehabilitation programs in Ireland have limited coverage, and many patients cannot obtain systematic rehabilitation training, affecting quality of life and prognosis.

 

**Solution:** Large Chinese respiratory centers provide comprehensive pulmonary rehabilitation services, including multi-dimensional interventions such as exercise training, respiratory muscle exercises, nutritional guidance, and psychological support. Aether Dragon Medical can assist patients in obtaining systematic pulmonary rehabilitation treatment.
